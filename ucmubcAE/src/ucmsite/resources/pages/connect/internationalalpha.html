International Alpha
Hello. Ni Hao. Annyong ha shimnikka. Konichiwa. Hola. Hi. Our International Alpha provides international students who are ESL speakers an opportunity to learn English, meet Canadian and other students from around the world and explore Christianity. We will look at questions such as "Is Christianity Relevant for Today?" and "What is the Purpose of Life?" A delicious free dinner is followed by a short talk and then a discussion.
