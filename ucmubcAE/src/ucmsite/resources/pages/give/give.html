Giving to UCM
University Christian Ministry at UBC is situated on a campus of over 49,000 students, faculty and staff. People come from all over the world to work and study at this University. Statistics show that Vancouver has the least number of people who regularly attend a church in all of North America. The further you travel West, the less churched people are. With UBC situated on the most Western part of our city, UCM is ministering to one of the least evangelized mission fields in North America. Like many ministry organizations, UCM depends upon the support of individuals and churches in order for us to do the mission God has called us to on the UBC Campus. HOW DO I SUPPORT UCM? You can support UCM through prayer and by giving financially toward this ministry. The salaries of our staff and interns depend entirely upon the consistent giving of individuals and churches. Additionally, we are required to raise finances annually for our office and ministry expenses, and each year our student executive prayerfully set a budget for the ministry needs of their student initiatives. Below are three convenient ways you can support UCM: (1) GIVE ONLINE: You can make a contribution online by using your credit card.
