Alumni
Over the last five years we have seen almost 100 students from the UCM community graduate from UBC. Similar to our desire to be a relationally based ministry, our desire is to maintain relationships with those who have been sent out of our ministry. It is our vision to stay connected with these students, as ministers in the marketplace and the world, to encourage them with good news about the ongoing ministry at UBC and to inform them of how they can best continue to support this ministry. Here are some ways to stay connected after you have left university: UCM NEWSLETTER Our staff send out a ministry newsletter each quarter. If you would like to receive this newsletter by mail or by email, please let us know. ubcstaff@ucmonline.ca FACEBOOK ALUMNI GROUP Join our UCM Alumni http://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=70214447083 group to stay connected with those near and a far. UCM REUNION On May 23rd, 2009 we hosted our first UCM Reunion and we had a wonderful time reconnecting with old friends and hearing about the journey UCM has been on over the last 5 years. We hope to host another reunion in the future and reconnect with another generation of UCM alumni.
